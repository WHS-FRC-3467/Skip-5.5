Elevator Commands:

elevatorCalibrate - Zero elevator encoder

elevatorDrive - Drive elevator manually, either by joystick or by fixed rate.

elevatorHoldLevel - drive to an elevator level and stay there until interrupted

elevatorLevelsUpDown - drive up or down to next level and stay there until interrupted

elevatorToPosition - drive to specified encoder position and stay there until interrupted

elevatorUpdatePIDF - Command used by SmartDashboard to update elevator PIDF constants
